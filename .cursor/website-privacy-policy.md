**Privacy Policy**

SONDR collects the information you provide when you create an account (such as your email and username), as well as the habits, tasks, and progress data you add in the app. We use this data to run the app, sync your content across devices, and—when you add friends—to show your chosen progress to those friends in their feed. Profile information and any photos you upload are stored securely using industry-standard services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we use your information only to provide, improve, and secure the SONDR experience. By using SONDR, you agree to this privacy policy.
